About Us

Botswana's Biggest University

Chemutengure University is a distinguished higher education institution in Botswana, renowned for its specialized focus on business and computing programs. As the demand for skilled professionals in these fields grows globally, Chemutengure stands out for its commitment to providing quality education tailored to industry needs.
The university offers a diverse range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including Business Administration, Accounting, Information Technology, and Computer Science.
These programs are designed to equip students with both theoretical knowledge and practical skills, ensuring they graduate as competent professionals ready to contribute to Botswana’s economy.
One of Chemutengure’s key strengths is its modern infrastructure, which includes state-of-the-art computer labs, business simulation facilities, and a well-resourced library.
The university also emphasizes research and innovation, encouraging students and faculty to engage in projects that address real-world challenges in business and technology.Beyond academics, Chemutengure fosters a vibrant campus life with student clubs, entrepreneurship workshops, and industry partnerships that provide internship and networking opportunities.
These initiatives prepare students not only for employment but also for leadership roles in their respective fields.As Botswana advances toward a digital and knowledge-driven future, Chemutengure University plays a pivotal role in shaping the next generation of business and computing professionals. Its dedication to excellence ensures that graduates are well-prepared to thrive in a competitive global landscape.
